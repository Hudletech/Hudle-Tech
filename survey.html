Insider Tips and Must-Have Knowledge

Before we begin the survey, here are a few things to know before owning a website.

1. Purpose


When it comes to owning a website, it is important to have a clear understanding of it's purpose. Knowning the reason behind creating your website will help guide the design, content, and overall direction of your online presence.


2. Domain and Hosting


Choose a domain name that reflects your brand and is easy to remember. Research different hosting options to find one that suites your needs interm of storage, bandwidth, and customer support.



3. Design and User Experience


Invest in a visually appealing and user-friendly design. Make sure your website is responsive, meaning it looks good and functions well on different devices.


4. Security


Protect your website from cyber threats by using an ssl certificate.


5. Analytics


Setting up websites analytics helps to track visitors behaviour, traffic sources, and other valuable insights. This data will help you optimize your website and make informed decisions.



Reminder!

Owning a website requires ongoing maintenance and updates. Stay up to date with industry trends and keep your content fresh to ensure a successful online presence.
