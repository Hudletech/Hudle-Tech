Our Policies On Web Development!

At Hudle Technology, we believe in clear and transparent policies to ensure a smooth and successful web development journey for our clients. Here are some key points to know.
This only counts when the mistakes are on our end and not yours!
1. Website Revisions:

Complimentary revisions to address any errors that may have occurred during the development process. we understand that mistakes happen,and we are committed to ensuring your website is error free and meets up to your expectations.

More functionality = More money!
2. Modification Requests:

We want to make sure you are aware that any additional requirements or changes requested after the development phase has begun or been completed will incur additional charges. we are more than happy to accommodate your needs, but it is important to consider the extra time and effort required for these additional requests.

No payment, No service!
3. Payment Terms:

We have a payment policy where we require the full payment before the development process can begin. This is because we use a portion of the funds to purchase the domain name for your website and pay for the server hosting. By completing the payment upfront, we can ensure a smooth and efficient development journey for your site.

Simple projects = Less time
Complex projects = More time!
4. Time Duration:

The standard time duration for a site is usually around one month. However, the actual completion time can vary depending on the simplicity or complexity of your specific site. For simpler websites we may be able to finish within three weeks, while more intricate projects might take a bit longer, possibly extending beyond a month.
